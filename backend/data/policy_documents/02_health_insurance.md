# Health Insurance

## Overview
Health insurance covers medical expenses incurred due to illnesses or accidents. It reimburses hospitalization costs, day-care procedures, and pre/post hospitalization expenses. Health insurance is essential for protecting savings against medical emergencies.

## Key Features
- Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals
- Reimbursement claims at non-network hospitals
- Coverage for pre-hospitalization (30-60 days) and post-hospitalization (60-90 days) expenses
- No claim bonus (NCB) - increase in coverage for every claim-free year
- Day-care procedures covered (no 24-hour hospitalization required)
- Tax benefit under Section 80D

## Types of Health Insurance
1. Individual Health Insurance - Covers one person
2. Family Floater Health Insurance - Covers entire family under single sum insured
3. Senior Citizen Health Insurance - For ages 60+ with higher premiums
4. Critical Illness Insurance - Lump sum payout on diagnosis of specified illnesses
5. Group Health Insurance - Provided by employers to employees
6. Top-up Health Plans - Additional coverage above base plan

## Coverage Details
- Room rent (usually up to 1-2% of sum insured per day)
- ICU charges
- Doctor's consultation fees
- Medicine and diagnostic costs
- Ambulance charges (usually up to Rs. 2000-5000)
- Organ donor expenses
- Pre-existing disease coverage after waiting period (usually 2-4 years)

## Exclusions
- Pre-existing diseases during waiting period (typically first 2-4 years)
- Cosmetic and plastic surgery (unless medically necessary)
- Dental treatment (unless accident-related)
- War, nuclear risks, terrorism
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Unani (unless specifically covered)
- 30-day waiting period from policy start for most illnesses

## Waiting Periods
- Initial waiting period: 30 days from policy start
- Pre-existing disease waiting period: 2-4 years
- Specific disease waiting period: 1-2 years (cataract, hernia, etc.)

## Claim Settlement Ratio
- Star Health: 95%+ claim settlement ratio
- Apollo Munich: 94%+ 
- HDFC Ergo: 93%+
- ICICI Lombard: 92%+
- New India Assurance: 90%+

## Ideal For
- Individuals and families without employer coverage
- Self-employed professionals
- Senior citizens (dedicated senior citizen plans)
- People with family history of medical conditions
